Our Mission to Reduce Food Waste

FoodSave connects restaurants with surplus food to customers at discounted prices, reducing waste while helping you save money.

Why We Exist

1.3 billion tons of food is wasted globally each year while millions go hungry.

Founded in 2020, FoodSave was created to address this paradox. We believe good food should be eaten, not wasted. Our platform connects restaurants with surplus food to customers looking for great deals, creating a win-win for businesses, consumers, and the planet.
