Cookie Policy

This policy explains how we use cookies and similar technologies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalization
  • Targeting Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads to you

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
session_id Maintains your session state across page requests Session
user_preferences Stores your language and display preferences 1 year
_ga Google Analytics - distinguishes unique users 2 years
_gid Google Analytics - distinguishes unique users 24 hours
fbp Facebook Pixel - tracks conversions from Facebook ads 3 months

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics of the Service, deliver advertisements on and through the Service, and so on.

Some of these third-party services may include:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Hotjar
  • Stripe (for payment processing)

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

To manage your cookie preferences:

  • Adjust your browser settings (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Use our cookie consent tool (available in the footer of our website)
  • Opt-out of third-party cookies through their respective websites
